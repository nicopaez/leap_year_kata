Leap Year Kata
===============

A year is leap when it is divisible by 4 and is not divisible by 100 except it is also divisable by 400.
Some examples:

* 1900
  is divisible by 4
  is divisible by 100
  is NOT divisible by 400
  => is NOT leap

* 1996
  is divisible by 4
  is NOT divisible by 100
  => IS leap

* 2000
  is divisible by 4
  is divisible by 100
  is divisible by 400
  => IS leap

* 2001
  it NOT divisible by 4
  => is NOT leap
